About

Built by an engineer,
not a salesperson

I'm Chris Callaway — founder of Gazellia and the engineer behind every system we build. I've spent 13+ years writing production software, and I still write code every single day.

I started Gazellia because I saw something broken in the industry: companies spending six figures on development agencies that ship mediocre code slowly. I knew AI could change that equation — not by replacing developers, but by giving businesses access to intelligent systems that actually get work done.

Before Gazellia, I built the web scraping engine at Fyre — a recruiting startup that was acquired by Bullhorn and now serves thousands of customers. I co-founded Cloudgate, a recruiting platform built from scratch. I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a 4.0 in Computer Science and worked on augmented reality projects for Google Glass before AR was mainstream.

Today, I run a team of AI agents and human developers that handle everything from autonomous code generation to AI-powered marketing. The agents you see on this site aren't concepts — they're running right now, completing tickets, optimizing campaigns, and getting smarter with every task.
